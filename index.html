Waa'ee Koo & Jireenya Koo

Mul'ata Boruu: Amma hojii haaraa uumuu fi of ijaaruu irratti xiyyeeffadhee jira. Boru garuu, hiriyaa jaalalaa tan amala koof taatu, tan har'a hiriyaa, boru immoo Haadha Manaa koo taatee jireenya koo waliin miidhagsitu argachuun abjuu kooti.
